What Are Fly Ash Bricks?
Fly ash bricks are an eco-friendly alternative to traditional clay bricks, made from a blend of fly ash, cement, and water. These bricks are lightweight, durable, and offer superior thermal insulation, making them an ideal choice for modern construction.
